Instruction to Authors Manuscripts for confluence 2023 will ONLY be accepted in electronic format through Easy Chair online submission system. Do not send hardcopy of your paper. If you are unable to submit your contribution electronically, please contact us.

  • Authors should only submit originally written, unpublished work to confluence 2023.
  • Submissions should strictly follow the recommended IEEE conference template.
  • All references must follow the IEEE format of referencing
  • To facilitate the paper's double-blind peer review process, authors should make efforts to ensure that information about the authors' identities do not appear anywhere in the text-body of the paper. (for the first submission)
  • Please limit your paper within 6 pages, and the size of your PDF file less than 2MB, files larger than 2MB will not be accepted for upload.
  • Authors wishing to submit an over-length manuscript may do so. There will be an over-length page fee of $25 per page and limited to an additional 2 pages (maximum page submission is eight (8) pages). This payment will be required after
  • All fonts must be embedded in the file.
  • Fonts that require non-English language support are not allowed.
  • The document should not have any password protection.
  • Do not put your own page numbers on the manuscript.
  • The decision regarding the acceptance of the papers is at the discretion of the Technical Program Committee.
  • Note that short manuscripts / abstract are not considered
