Transportation

Nearest Airport

Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi, India. International flights and some domestic flights arrive at Terminal 3 and Terminal 1. Prepaid taxis, luxury cabs are available at the airport to commute across the city.

Railway Stations

Nizamuddin Railway Station and Anand Vihar Railway Station is closest to the venue of the conference. Other railway stations are Old Delhi Railway Station, New Delhi Railway Station. From all stations prepaid three wheelers and taxis are available.

Weather

Weather of Noida is similar to Delhi. January is the mid of winter season, winter starts from October therefore weather is cold.
